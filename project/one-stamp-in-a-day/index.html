One Stamp In A Day

Capturing and telling stories like this one was what we first set out to do. Meeting Masa and seeing him work was truly inspiring. When we saw his website, we immediately knew this was a story we wanted to tell and show the world.

Masa comes from Japan and is currently living in the Hague, the Netherlands. He has been educated in retail management but followed his passion to create. He worked as an architect and graphic designer. Working these two jobs he stumbled into making stamps for people. He is “creating something nobody has ever seen before”. If you want to know more about Masaaki and his stamps, take a look at his website One Stamp In A Day. Or go to his personal website.

The music in the video was created by Roberto Garretón from 360° Sound.
