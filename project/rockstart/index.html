Rockstart

“Step forward. Start.” That is how the Rockstart manifesto ends, so that is what we did. For 5 months we had the opportunity to work with this great company in Amsterdam. During our time we created some video productions and got to tell the Rockstart story and the stories of the startups around them.

We experienced the Rockstart accelerator program from start, all the way up to the big Demo Day. We created the video of this intense presentation day full of passionate startups, potential investors and influencers in the Dutch startup scene.

Rockstart helped us step into a new world full of passionate people with great stories all around them. So we would like to say thank you, you rock!
